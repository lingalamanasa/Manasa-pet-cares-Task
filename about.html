Our Mission

We Believe Every Pet Deserves the Best Care

At Stackly, we're more than just a pet care company. We're a community of passionate animal lovers, veterinarians, and pet wellness experts who believe that every pet deserves premium care, regardless of breed or size.

Founded in 2019, we started with a simple belief: pets are family. By combining expert veterinary knowledge with the finest products sourced globally, we've built a brand that pet parents trust and veterinarians recommend.
